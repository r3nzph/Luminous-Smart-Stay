✏ About
About Us

An illuminated destination of prestige, warmth, and breathtaking views.

A symbol of timeless elegance rising between the calming ocean horizon and the dazzling city skyline. Inspired by modern European luxury and refined hospitality, our hotel blends sophistication, comfort, and breathtaking views into one unforgettable experience.

Located in a prime destination, Luminous Smart Stay offers panoramic ocean views at the back and spectacular city lights at the front — giving our guests the best of both worlds. Our signature red and gold interiors represent passion, prestige, and warmth, ensuring every stay feels exclusive yet welcoming.

Whether you are traveling for leisure, business, romance, or celebration, Luminous Smart Stay is designed to provide a seamless and luxurious escape. From our rooftop infinity pool to our 360° Sky Restaurant, every detail reflects our commitment to excellence.

At Luminous Smart Stay, we don’t just offer rooms — we create illuminated experiences.
